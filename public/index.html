-WELCOME TO SEATTLE-

This is an app that will guide tourists to their desired locations; from iconic viewpoints (ideal for photos) to outdoor activities and city events. Apptivities will offer suggestions for how to move about the city from bicycle rentals and bus schedules (one bus away app), to light rails and water taxis to Alki Beach.
